  en: |
    Our Beloved Church is a mobile web application designed to support daily faith practices within church communities. The system enables members to record and manage activities such as scripture memorization, prayer, and meditation.

    Church leaders can access individual- and group-level statistics through an administrative interface, allowing data-informed pastoral guidance and communication. The application also supports announcements and multimedia content delivery, facilitating interaction between administrators and members via a centralized web-based database.

    This project covers concept planning, UX/UI design, and full-stack web development, with a strong emphasis on usability and accessibility across a wide age range.
