    An ERP-oriented internal management system designed to centrally manage staff information, participation records, and operational performance within a controlled organizational environment. The system was architected with a strong focus on security and operational reliability, incorporating IP-based access control, user activity tracking, and comprehensive logging mechanisms.

    These security-oriented design decisions enable strict access governance, traceability of user actions, and accountability across internal workflows, making the platform suitable for managing sensitive organizational data in real-world operational contexts.

  kr: |
